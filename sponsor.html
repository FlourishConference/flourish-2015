Sponsor Flourish

For eight years, Flourish has been Chicago’s free open source conference. Flourish can only afford to create an amazing conference, and bring together the vibrant Chicago open source community through your contributions. As a sponsor, you'll be able to introduce yourself to the talent of the community while also becoming a member of the community.

What follows is a guide of what you can expect from your sponsorship. Any questions, concerns, potential substitutions can be sent to the Flourish Committee by email at info@flourishconf.com.

Diamond Sponsor

$3000

  • Logo featured on the Flourish website, and prominently featured on all promotional materials.
  • Approved materials distributed to all attendees at registration.
  • Full page advertisement in the conference program.
  • Direct sponsorship of a segment of the conference (ex. After Party sponsored by [Company])

Platinum Sponsor

$2000

  • Logo featured on the Flourish website, and all promotional materials.
  • Approved materials distributed to all attendees at registration.
  • Half page advertisement in conference program.
  • Direct sponsorship of a segment of the conference (ex. Lunch sponsored by [Company])

  • Half page advertisement in conference program.
